甲骨文Ubuntu搭建EMBY与挂载google drive

· 2022-06-01 ·

一、安装emby
1、sudo -i
2、安装emby： wget https://github.com/MediaBrowser/Emby.Releases/releases/download/4.7.2.0/emby-server-deb_4.7.2.0_arm64.deb
3、dpkg -i emby-server-deb_4.7.2.0_arm64.deb
4、访问： ip:8096
注意：如果你还是打不开那可能是因为端口未开放的缘故 Ubuntu系统下打开端口 iptables -F

二、挂载google drive
1、安装rclone
注意：最好选择低版本，下载连接：https://downloads.rclone.org/v1.57.0/
最新版本：curl https://rclone.org/install.sh | sudo bash
2、配置

3、挂载
mkdir -p /home/gdrive

/usr/bin/rclone mount emby: /home/gdrive
--umask 0000
--default-permissions
--allow-non-empty
--allow-other
--buffer-size 32M
--dir-cache-time 12h
--vfs-read-chunk-size 64M
--vfs-read-chunk-size-limit 1G &

4、查看挂载
df -h

5、自动挂载
cat > /etc/systemd/system/rclone.service <<EOF
[Unit]
Description=Rclone
AssertPathIsDirectory=LocalFolder
After=network-online.target

[Service]
Type=simple
ExecStart=/usr/bin/rclone mount emby: /home/gdrive
--umask 0000
--default-permissions
--allow-non-empty
--allow-other
--buffer-size 32M
--dir-cache-time 12h
--vfs-read-chunk-size 64M
--vfs-read-chunk-size-limit 1G
ExecStop=/bin/fusermount -u LocalFolder
Restart=on-abort
User=root

[Install]
WantedBy=default.target
EOF

6、设置启动
systemctl start rclone

7、开启启动
systemctl enable rclone

8、虚拟内存
wget https://www.moerats.com/usr/shell/swap.sh && bash swap.sh
